Binary Search in Plain English

DSA

Binary search is "guess the middle, keep the half that could contain the answer." The skeleton:

lo, hi = 0, n - 1
while lo <= hi:
    mid = (lo + hi) // 2
    if good(mid): hi = mid - 1  # or move lo depending on monotonic 'good'
    else:          lo = mid + 1

Works whenever the answer lies on a monotonic boundary: once something becomes true, it stays true, or vice versa.
