Overfitting and Regularization - a friendly guide

ML

Overfitting is memorizing the training set quirks. Fixes:

  • Get better data, cleaning beats modeling.
  • Use simpler models or fewer parameters.
  • Add regularization (L2, dropout), early stopping, or data augmentation.
  • Measure on a validation split and iterate.
