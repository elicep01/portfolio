Gradient Descent - Intuition First

ML

If loss is the height of a hill, the gradient is the direction of steepest ascent. Step in the opposite direction by a small amount, the learning rate. Repeat until the slope is near zero.

Troubleshooting: diverging? lower LR. crawling? raise LR or add momentum. stuck in plateaus? try normalization or a better init.
